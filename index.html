About Us

Dynable is a platform which allows consumers with dietary restrictions to eat without worry. Through our service, an individual or group of people will have the ability to input foods that they wish to avoid and retrieve restaurant and meal options from a database that fit the necessary requirements. In addition, users can filter restaurants by location, type of food, price, special offers, ingredient, rating, and brand. Our goal is to allow those with dietary restrictions to enjoy their meal, and know that they are getting exactly what they want.
